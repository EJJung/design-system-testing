What is ResearchDataGov?

ResearchDataGov is a web portal for discovering and requesting access to restricted microdata from federal statistical agencies. RDG advances the evidence-building capabilities of the Federal government, which was a key goal of the Foundations for Evidence-Based Policymaking Act of 2018.
